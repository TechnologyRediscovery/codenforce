Hark! No valid authorization periods found

You have successfully logged in, but your user account is not authorized at any level--in any municipality. Unfortunately, this error must be corrected by an authorized user.

If you have never successfully loaded a system dashboard before, your administrator probably didn't configure your UserAuthorizationPeriod correctly. If you've previously been authorized in the system, then it's likely that your authorization periods have all expired. In rare cases, all of your previously valid authorization periods have been deliberately invalidated.
