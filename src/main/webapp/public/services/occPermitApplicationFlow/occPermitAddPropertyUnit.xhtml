If you would like to apply for an occupancy permit in your municipality, please begin the process below to submit your claim.
3. Configure unit list

Before applying for occupancy, please create a complete list of all the units in the property, including those that you do not want to apply for. Press "Add Unit" to add an empty unit, and click the pencil in the edit column to fill out the information. The Unit Number is required, a description may be left blank. The description should just describe what the room is (e.g. basement). If you intend to rent please explain your plans under "Rental notes".
