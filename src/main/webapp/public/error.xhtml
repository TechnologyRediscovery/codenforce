Troubleshooting

A common source of page load errors may be not having an appropriate business object activated when trying to view its details (i.e. trying to view a Property's profile without having searched for and selected an active property.

Check the entries in your system status bar above for details on which Property, Case, Municipality, etc., are active during your session.
